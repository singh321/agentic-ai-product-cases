# AI Meeting Prep

## Problem
Sales and product teams enter meetings without sufficient context.

---

## Goal
Provide AI-generated meeting insights inside inbox/calendar.

---

## Workflow

1. detect upcoming meeting
2. fetch participant data
3. enrich company + person info
4. generate summary
5. suggest talking points

---

## System Design

- calendar integration
- CRM integration
- enrichment APIs
- LLM summarization layer

---

## Key Challenges

- identifying important meetings
- filtering noise
- generating useful insights

---

## Evaluation

- relevance score
- usage rate
- meeting success feedback

---

## Metrics

- feature adoption
- meeting prep usage
- deal conversion impact

---

## Insight

> Precision is more important than recall for meeting prep.
